Project Overview

A redesign focused on brand expression and a more persuasive customer journey.

Challenge

The original storefront looked template-driven, with limited brand personality and a weaker visual hierarchy across key sections.

Direction

The redesign introduces more deliberate typography, improved spacing, and a clearer customer journey to create a stronger brand impression.

Outcome

The final result feels more like a polished boutique storefront than a default catalog template.

Before and After

A visual transformation designed to stop the scroll and feel more premium at first glance.

Before

The earlier website lacked distinction and a clear promotional rhythm.

The original site relied on a standard storefront structure with tighter spacing, weaker hierarchy, and a look that did not fully support the brand's personality.

  • Template-driven visual feel
  • Less memorable homepage storytelling
  • Weaker emphasis on branded promotion
After

The refreshed direction improves clarity, polish, and campaign presence.

The updated concept brings a stronger hero composition, better section pacing, and a more intentional boutique feel that immediately reads as more custom and brand-aware.

  • Sharper typography and content hierarchy
  • Cleaner visual flow across sections
  • More premium and editorial presentation
