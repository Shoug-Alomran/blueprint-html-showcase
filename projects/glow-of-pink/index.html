Glow of Pink

Before to after: a clearer, more branded website transformation.

This case study presents how a generic storefront experience can be reshaped into a sharper brand-led website with stronger storytelling, more intentional layout pacing, and cleaner promotional presentation.

Project Overview

A redesign focused on brand expression and a more persuasive customer journey.

Challenge

The original experience felt template-driven, with limited visual personality and weak hierarchy across promotional content blocks.

Direction

The redesign introduces more intentional typography, elevated spacing, and a cleaner content sequence to strengthen the brand impression.

Outcome

The updated concept reads more like a focused boutique landing page rather than a default catalog layout.
